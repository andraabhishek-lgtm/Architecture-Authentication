Behind the Scenes

The Story Behind Stackly

We're a team of architects, designers, and engineers who spent years wrestling with the same operational chaos. In 2023, we decided to fix it.

From the drawing board to digital clarity

Stackly began in a small studio in Bengaluru where three architects were juggling 30 active projects across WhatsApp groups, Google Drive folders, and multiple invoicing tools. The breaking point came when a revision request got lost in email, delaying a flagship project by three weeks.

We built the first version of Stackly in six months — a simple client portal connected to a studio dashboard. Today it powers over 1,200 projects across 12 cities.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Stackly. Can't find your answer? Contact our team →

What is Stackly and who is it for?
Stackly is a project management and client communication platform built specifically for architecture studios. It is designed for two types of users: Admin (studio owners, project managers, architects) who manage projects, clients, and invoices; and Clients who want real-time visibility into their project's progress, design documents, and billing. Any architecture firm — from solo practitioners to large studios — can use Stackly.
How do I get started with Stackly?
Getting started is simple. Click "Get Started" on the home page, fill in your details, and choose your role (Admin or Client). If you're an admin, you'll be taken to your studio dashboard where you can create your first project and invite your client. Clients receive an invitation link to create their account and access their portal. The whole setup takes less than five minutes.
Is my project data secure on Stackly?
Yes. All data on Stackly is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Project files, design documents, invoices, and client communications are stored in isolated environments. We never share your data with third parties and we conduct regular security audits. Role-based access ensures that clients can only see their own project information, and admins control what is visible to each client.
Can my clients access Stackly without a technical background?
Absolutely. The client portal is designed to be as intuitive as possible — clients can view their project progress, review design documents, check invoice status, and raise support tickets with no technical knowledge required. We routinely test usability with non-technical users to ensure the experience is clear and accessible across all devices including mobile phones.
What types of files can I share through Stackly?
Stackly supports a wide range of file types commonly used in architecture: PDF drawings, AutoCAD files (.dwg, .dxf), 3D renders (PNG, JPEG, TIFF), presentation decks, and Excel/CSV spreadsheets for schedules and budgets. Files are linked directly to the relevant project milestone so clients always know what they're reviewing.
How does the invoicing feature work?
Admins can create professional invoices tied to specific project milestones or general billing cycles. Each invoice shows the amount, due date, line items, and status (Paid / Due / Overdue). Clients can view all their invoices in their portal. Admins can track overall revenue and outstanding amounts in the dashboard's Revenue section. Payment integration with popular Indian gateways is on our roadmap.
Is Stackly available on mobile?
Yes. Stackly is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes — phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. No app download is required; the web platform adapts automatically. Mobile-specific features like collapsible sidebars, touch-friendly navigation, and optimised layouts are built into every page so both admins and clients can stay up to date on the go.
How is pricing structured?
We offer a free tier for individual architects managing up to 3 active projects and 5 clients. Professional plans for growing studios include unlimited projects, custom branding, advanced reports, and priority support. Enterprise plans for large firms include dedicated account management and custom integrations. All plans are billed monthly with no lock-in. Full pricing details are available on our pricing page.
